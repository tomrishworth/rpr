About RPR Pacific

RPR Pacific is a specialist human resource consultancy focusing on international executive search, recruitment services and the provision of contract staff throughout the Pacific region.

Peter Rishworth is the Managing Director of RPR Pacific and has over 30 years experience in the search and selection of quality candidates for all types of management, technical and operational roles within companies, professional organisations and government agencies.

Based in Auckland, New Zealand, he established R.P.Rishworth & Co in 1985, which quickly became one of the leading international recruitment consultancies in the Pacific region.

In 1987 the company set up a branch office in Sydney, Australia and established another separate division specialising exclusively in International Health and Social Development projects, managing large contracts in Papua New Guinea funded by the Asian Development Bank and other New Zealand Government aid programmes in the Pacific Islands.

In 1993 Peter established Rishworth Aviation in Auckland, which became a world leader in the leasing of pilots to major airlines throughout the world and with a branch office in Stockholm, Sweden. The company was sold in 2005.

Through his company, RPR Pacific, Peter and his associates are able to assist their clients to find the best candidates worldwide, with a specialised expertise and focus within the aviation industry.

RPR Pacific Services

Executive Search

There is a constant need for high-performing executives and finding the right people can be an extremely difficult, costly and risky task for management. RPR Pacific has the experience and skills to identify the best candidates worldwide who will fit with your organisation. Our Executive Search process will include defining the search priorities and strategies, attracting and evaluating suitable candidates, presenting a short-list for client interviews and guiding the client through the final selection and contract negotiations.

Recruitment

RPR Pacific can source quality candidates for your organisation through our international associates and database, our own network and referrals, and through online and print advertising including social media. Our candidate verification process is extensive.

Contractors

RPR Pacific can provide a range of contract staff if this is the preferred solution for long or short term positions, to cover specific projects or peak seasonal requirements. There are many benefits to employers and contractors in using this option.
