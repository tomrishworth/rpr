About RPR Pacific

Peter Rishworth is the Managing Director of RPR Pacific and has over 30 years experience in the search and selection of quality candidates for all types of management, technical and operational roles within companies, professional organisations and government agencies throughout the Asia/Pacific region.

Based in Auckland, New Zealand, he established R.P.Rishworth & Co Ltd. in 1985, which quickly became one of the leading international recruitment consultancies in the Asia/Pacific region.

In 1987 the company set up a branch office in Sydney, Australia and established another separate division specialising exclusively in International Health and Social Development projects, managing large contracts in Papua New Guinea funded by the Asian Development Bank and other New Zealand Government aid programmes in the Pacific Islands.

In 1993 Peter established Rishworth Aviation in Auckland, which has since become a world leader in the leasing of pilots to major airlines throughout the world and with a branch office in Stockholm, Sweden. The company was sold in 2005.

Through his new company, RPR Pacific, Peter and his associates are available to assist clients to find the best people worldwide in all disciplines and industries with a special interest in the aviation industry.
